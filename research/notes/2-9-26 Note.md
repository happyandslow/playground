


### QKV fusion 
- Merging local Xq, Xk, Xv
- allReduce + broadcast along y axis
- 3 * dim_p_pe * bsz

### Scoring 
-  score = Xq * K
- [1, dim_p_pe] * [dim_p_pe, seq_len_local]
- allReduce + broadcast along x axis 

### Softmax
- AllReduce/broadcast along y axis

### Output
- output =  V * score
- [dim_p_pe, seq_len_local] * [1, dim_p_pe]
- AllReduce/broadcast along y axis
